Low diametral interferences are intended for applications involving low to medium torque
and low internal pressures.
Medium diametral interferences are used in applications where the carbide cylinder is
subjected to internal pressure.
The compressive pre-stress applied to the carbide by the
interference ﬁt must be large enough to keep the carbide in compression throughout the
working cycle and not allow it to be exposed to tensile stress.
